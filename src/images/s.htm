Taurus (April 20 - May 20)

The Ruling Planet is Venus, the planet of beauty and love

Vibe: Determined energy

Good Traits:

  • Patient
  • organized
  • supportive
  • romantic
  • careful
  • dedicated

Bad Traits:

  • Overindulgent
  • stubborn
  • lazy
  • vain
  • cheap
  • too cautious

Secret Wishes:

  • To own the best of everything

Hates

Being rushed, wasting money, dirty things, hotels, mornings,

Favorites

Photography, the mountains, great music, rich/gourmet food, satin sheets,

Mental Traits

People born under this sign love nice things; they can come across as extremely materialistic, or as people who enjoy the finer things in life. They are sometimes slow to act, great at managing money, lovers of the outdoors (and of doing outdoor activities such as farming or gardening), very practical and down to earth. Taurus people typically dislike change.
